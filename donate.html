Advertising Support

If you decide to advertise with CenterStage Band and Show Choir Inc., you will be shown on our Sponsors section of this website and your ad will appear in our annual fall show program. Approximate annual attendance at the Fall Show is 2,500 – that means that all of these patrons will view your ad, which is excellent coverage for your advertising dollar. At the same time, you will be shown as being supportive of the arts in our community. In appreciation, you will receive ticket vouchers redeemable for two tickets at each evening’s performance during the annual fall show. Our advertising rates are very reasonable with several different sizes and layouts available. Special Support There are many reasons to support CenterStage Band and Show Choir Inc. with a donation. We are a community band and show choir made up of your friends, family, neighbors, and coworkers. CenterStage is a 501(c) (3) non-profit organization, so your gift is tax deductible.

General Fund

Since tickets to the fall show are free, we rely on generous donations to meet operating expenses and plan for the future. Through sound fiscal management and countless volunteer hours, CenterStage is ramping up to be a strong and viable organization for years to come. Your donation to this fund helps CenterStage broaden and support vocal and instrumental arts in our community.

Match Your Gift

If your place of employment has a program to match charitable contributions, it could double the value of your gift to CenterStage.
This can be achieved in three easy steps:
  • Ask your employer if they have a gift-matching program.
  • Make your donation to CenterStage.
  • Take your CenterStage donation receipt to your employer.
